These instructional videos, are only intended for graduates of the Pacific Center for Awareness and Bodywork, or other Bodyworkers trained in Structural Integration. They are not intended for novices.

It is with great joy, and in honor of Lee Joseph, that this DVD set is being offered. The ten sessions of Awareness Oriented Structural Integration, in which you will see Lee working with several clients over the course of several months, was filmed in 2014. In April of 2015 Lee was diagnosed with colon cancer, and died on August 17, just four and a half months later.

Lee was a vibrant, master teacher, full of love and passion for his work and life. He exuded presence and his teaching was always instilled with compassion, humor and commitment to going beyond the story and connecting with the greater Awareness that we are.

It is my hope that this DVD series will impart some of his knowledge and essence to those of you who watch it, and perhaps be a sweet remembrance to those of you who experienced the gift of being in the classroom with him. He is greatly missed.

With Love and grateful appreciation,
Carole Madsen
